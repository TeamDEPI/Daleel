About Elena Petrova

Hello, I'm Elena, a passionate historian and licensed tour guide deeply in love with the eternal city of Rome. For over 10 years, I’ve had the privilege of sharing Rome’s incredible history, art, and vibrant culture with visitors from all over the world. My tours are designed to be immersive, engaging, and tailored to your interests, whether you’re a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveler looking for hidden gems. Let’s explore Rome’s iconic landmarks, ancient ruins, and charming alleyways together, uncovering stories that bring the past to life!
