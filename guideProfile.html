About Me

As a lifelong resident of Egypt, I'm passionate about sharing the vibrant culture, rich history, and hidden gems of my beloved city. My tours are designed to be immersive and personal, moving beyond the typical tourist paths to uncover authentic experiences.

From the bustling souqs and modern architecture marvels to the serene desert landscapes, I'll guide you through Cairo's diverse tapestry. I specialize in culinary tours, art and culture explorations, and off-road desert adventures. My goal is not just to show you places, but to help you connect with the heart and soul of Egypt.

Join me for an unforgettable journey, whether you're a solo traveler, a family, or a group of friends. Let's create memories that will last a lifetime!
